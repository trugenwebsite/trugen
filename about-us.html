Who We Are

Trugen Pharmaceuticals Pvt Ltd. is a leading WHO-GMP manufacturing company, established in the year 2006. Our Corporate office is in New Delhi and our manufacturing facilities are situated in an open green space at Vill. Tejjupur, Near Chodiala Rly. Station, Roorkee, Distt. Haridwar (U.K.) INDIA, about 186 Kms from our capital New Delhi.

Trugen is working efficiently and effectively in this domain, gradually established as a renowned manufacturer and supplier of pharmaceutical drugs. Our organization aims at offering medicines, which result in the happy and healthy life with no risk and side effects.

We are a leading, next-generation Technology Company with strong business interests in Research molecules, Product Development, and host of cutting-edge technology services to improve the quality of life.
